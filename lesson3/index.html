Site Name

Weather Presents for Southeast Idaho


Site Purpose

Every day residents of three southeastern towns in Idaho (Preston, Soda Springs, Fish Haven) are presented with gifts from the sky in the form of weather.

Weather Presents for Southeast Idaho helps its viewers know 10 days in advance what weather presents are coming so they can plan their activities appropriately.

The site will also provide information about school closures due to weather, as well as radar and current conditions.
