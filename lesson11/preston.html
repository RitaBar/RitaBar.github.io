Mix and Mingle

Join with friends and family each week on Saturday. Pancakes will be served at the city park pavilion.

We gather every week. As an anonymous British poet said:
"Whether the weather be fine
Or whether the weather be not,
Whether the weather be cold
Or whether the weather be hot,
We'll weather the weather
Whatever the weather,
Whether we like it or not."

This weekly gathering began as an opportunity to meet with city leaders. While it still provides this type of discussions, it also provides a way to meet with old friends and find new ones.

Come join us.
