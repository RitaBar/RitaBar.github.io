Snowmobiling

Getting out into nature, whatever the season, provides a wonderful experience. In the winter some people enjoy walking, others skiing. Then there are those who love to go snowmobiling.

It’s easy and quick to learn to drive a snowmobile. This is a sport that people of a wide variety of ages can participate in while building strong ties with friends and family.

There’s a wonderful area nearby. Just head west on Fish Haven Canyon Rd 1.2 miles to parking area.

Enjoy seeing places familiar and new areas covered in snow.
