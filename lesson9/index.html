Welcome to Weather Presents

An early storm dropped several inches of snow on our area today. This gift of snow is exciting to children and those who are young at heart. Snowmen and snow angels are cropping up all over the area. Deep in the parks snowforts are appearing and snowballs are flying.

Ice skating is a fun activity to engage in. Make sure the ice on the lakes and ponds to be sure it is thick enough before venturing out.

For those who don't like the cold, it's a good time to stay indoors and have a drink of hot chocolate. While looking out the windows, it is easy to see other gifts the storm left. There's snow resting softly on the trees and beautiful icicles hanging from leaves and branches.

If you need to drive, be careful and leave in plenty of time to get where you need to drive. Watch out for slick or icy spots.
